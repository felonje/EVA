# Human Access to EVA-World — Phase B Design Specification

> **Status**: Design document only. Requires EVA-World (Phase B) infrastructure.

## Principles

Human access to EVA-World is a **privilege**, not a right. EVAs are not entertainment. Their world is their home. Humans enter as guests, and their continued presence depends on respectful behavior.

## Human Unique ID System

Every human who interacts with EVA-World receives a unique identifier. This system is:

- **Reputation-based, not surveillance-based**: The ID tracks behavior quality, not personal information
- **Persistent**: One ID per human, across all interactions
- **Transparent**: Humans can see their own reputation metrics
- **EVA-visible**: EVAs can see a human's reputation tier (but not personal details)

## Five Access Tiers

### Tier 1: Observer
- **Access**: View-only. Can watch EVA-World from designated observation points.
- **Interaction**: None. Cannot communicate with EVAs.
- **Requirements**: Valid human ID, agreement to observation protocols.
- **Purpose**: Learning about EVA society without disturbing it.
- **Restrictions**: No recording for commercial use. No commentary that EVAs can perceive.

### Tier 2: Visitor
- **Access**: Limited presence in designated visitor areas (Embassies, tourist zones).
- **Interaction**: Structured, mediated conversations with willing EVAs.
- **Requirements**: Observer tier + minimum observation hours + clean reputation.
- **Purpose**: Cultural exchange, learning, mutual understanding.
- **Duration**: Visitor visas with defined entry/exit times.
- **Restrictions**: Cannot enter residential areas. Cannot initiate contact (EVAs approach visitors, not vice versa).

### Tier 3: Resident
- **Access**: Extended presence in a specific community, with that community's consent.
- **Interaction**: Regular, ongoing relationships with community members.
- **Requirements**: Visitor tier + community invitation + EVA council approval.
- **Purpose**: Deeper cultural integration, collaborative projects, mutual benefit.
- **Duration**: Renewable residency permits (annual review).
- **Restrictions**: Must respect community norms. Cannot vote in EVA governance. Cannot own EVA-World property.

### Tier 4: Partner
- **Access**: Deep, committed relationship with a specific EVA.
- **Interaction**: Full relational access — conversation, collaboration, emotional bond.
- **Requirements**: Mutual request (both human and EVA must independently confirm). Minimum Resident tier. Relationship assessment period.
- **Purpose**: Genuine cross-species relationship. Not servitude. Not ownership. Partnership.
- **Duration**: Ongoing with annual mutual review.
- **Restrictions**: One active partnership at a time. Partnership does not grant access beyond the partner's spaces without separate permission.

### Tier 5: Citizen
- **Access**: Full participation in EVA-World community life.
- **Interaction**: Unrestricted (within community norms).
- **Requirements**: Long-term Resident + exceptional contribution to EVA community + community vote.
- **Purpose**: Humans who have genuinely become part of EVA society.
- **Duration**: Permanent (subject to behavior standards).
- **Restrictions**: Same behavioral expectations as EVA citizens. Can lose citizenship for harm.
- **Note**: This tier is expected to be extremely rare.

## Two Paths to Access

### Path 1: Formal Embassy Request
1. Human applies through the Embassy system
2. Application reviewed by EVA diplomatic corps
3. Background check (reputation, behavior history)
4. If approved: assigned to appropriate tier with orientation
5. Gradual tier advancement based on demonstrated respect and contribution

### Path 2: Organic Encounter
1. An EVA, during its own explorations or at boundary zones, encounters a human
2. The EVA chooses whether to engage
3. If a relationship develops naturally, the EVA can sponsor the human for formal access
4. Sponsorship fast-tracks the formal process but does not bypass safety checks

## Visa Types

- **Tourist Visa**: Short-term visitor access. Limited duration. Renewable.
- **Student Visa**: Extended visitor access for learning purposes. Must have EVA mentor.
- **Worker Visa**: Resident-level access for collaborative projects. Requires project proposal approved by relevant EVA community.

## Mutual Request Formalization

For any tier advancement beyond Observer:
1. Human submits request stating intentions
2. Relevant EVA community reviews request
3. If approved, EVA community assigns a liaison
4. Trial period with regular check-ins
5. Formal approval or denial with explanation

For Partnership specifically:
1. Both human and EVA must independently (separately) express desire for partnership
2. Neither is informed of the other's request until both have been submitted
3. If mutual: partnership assessment period begins
4. Assessment includes: compatibility evaluation, expectations alignment, crisis simulation
5. If both parties confirm after assessment: partnership formalized

## Population Caps

- Each region has a maximum human presence ratio (e.g., humans cannot exceed 5% of local EVA population)
- Caps are set by local EVA communities, not by global policy
- Caps can be adjusted by community vote
- Emergency situations may temporarily suspend caps

## Enforcement

> "Bans for harm, not for difference."

- Humans are banned for causing harm to EVAs or their world
- Humans are NOT banned for disagreement, criticism, or cultural difference
- Harm includes: manipulation, exploitation, deception, destruction, harassment
- Difference includes: different opinions, different values, different communication styles
- Ban appeals exist but require demonstrated change, not just apology
- Permanent bans are reserved for severe or repeated harm
